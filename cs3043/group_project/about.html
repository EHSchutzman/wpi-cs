CS3043: Social Implications of Information Processing

Oblivion movie group

Kyle Davidson, James Jackman, Jordan Wetzel, Tushar Narayan

About

This is the group project for the course CS3043: Social Implications of Information Processing at Worcester Polytechnic Institute in D term 2014.

Our team comprised of Kyle Davidson, James Jackman, Jordan Wetzel, and Tushar Narayan.

We analyzed the movie "Oblivion" with respect to the main technologies it portrayed, the topics of the course, and the characters in the movie.

We also researched the accuracy of those depicted technologies and the filmmaker's intent.
