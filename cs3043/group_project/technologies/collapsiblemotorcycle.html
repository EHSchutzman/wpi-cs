Technology: Compact Collapsible Motorcycle

Jack uses a compact collapsible motorcycle to explore terrain in the movie. This technology is available in the current day.

A patent for a Collapsible Motorcycle is available at http://www.google.com/patents/US5029666.

Two websites that sell foldable motorcycles are http://www.foldingmotorbike.com/ and http://www.diblasi.com/home.htm. These motorcycles do not offer the same compactness as that depicted in the movie.

Something more similar to the movie seems to be a part of the fictional realm for now. For example, see the concept at http://www.strangecosmos.com/content/item/14271.html.
