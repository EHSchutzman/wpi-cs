Technology: Binocular Technologies

There is a tower viewer (coin operated binoculars) in the movie. The general consensus is that binoculars were first created by Hans Lippershey in 1608 http://www.britannica.com/EBchecked/topic/342926/Hans-Lippershey. Optical zooming technology has had over 400 years to mature and develop. Now there are massive orbital space telescopes, such as the Hubble Telescope, that can peer into the deepest recesses of space http://asd.gsfc.nasa.gov/archive/hubble/.
