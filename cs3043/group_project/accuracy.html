Accuracy/Reality

Many technologies in Oblivion exist or are currently being researched. Fusion power hasn’t been harnessed yet; however, modern nuclear weapons have a hydrogen fusion stage. Fusion power is currently still being researched. There are already patents out on touchscreen guided vehicles. Foldable motorcycles exist and a collapsible motorcycles has been patented. Deadly unmanned flying drone technology exists. The US army has a large arsenal of UAVs. Human stasis is still a science fiction concept. Some scientists speculate that it could be plausible in the distant future. Successful cloning has occurred in animals. Human cloning is plausible; however, exact copying with augmented memories is still in the realm of science fiction. AI exists, but self-aware AI haven’t been developed yet. Optical zooming technology has been around since Galileo invented the telescope in 1609. Spray on skin is currently a technology under development. Australia practices some methods. Large space stations like the TET could be plausible in the distant future. Space to ground technology has been around for decades.
